
### Project Name
"Amazon Product Sales and Performance Dashboard"

### Project Description
This project is a comprehensive business intelligence dashboard designed to analyze and visualize Amazon sales and product performance data across multiple categories. The dashboard offers real-time insights into key sales metrics, product reviews, and trend analysis to assist stakeholders with data-driven decision-making.

#### Key Features
- **KPI Overview**: Displays high-level metrics including Year-To-Date (YTD) Sales, Quarter-To-Date (QTD) Sales, Products Sold, and YTD Reviews, allowing instant assessment of business performance.[1]
- **Trend Analysis**: Visualizes sales trends by month and week, making it simple to identify seasonal patterns or anomalies in sales activities.
- **Product Category Breakdown**: Highlights sales performance across different product categories, showing both percentages and absolute figures for YTD and QTD sales to pinpoint high-performing sectors.[1]
- **Top Products Identification**: Ranks top five products by sales and reviews, empowering product managers to recognize best-sellers and focus on customer feedback.
- **Interactive Slicers**: Allows users to filter data by year, quarter, and product category for flexible and targeted analysis, supporting a wide range of business queries.

#### Business Benefits
- Enhances transparency of sales and review metrics for leadership reporting and strategic planning.[1]
- Empowers teams to optimize inventory, marketing, and sales strategies based on real-time performance data.[1]
- Enables cross-functional analysis by category, time period, and product, leading to actionable insights for boosting profitability.[1]

This dashboard is ideal for sales analysts, category managers, and executives who require a streamlined and visually intuitive tool for tracking, comparing, and reporting Amazon product and sales performance.
